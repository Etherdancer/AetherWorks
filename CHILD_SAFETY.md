# Child Safety Policy (Pravila o sigurnosti djece)

**Plain language:** Clear Space has zero tolerance for any content that hurts, exploits, or endangers children. We will immediately report anyone who tries to share this kind of material to the police and child safety organizations.

**Hrvatski:** Clear Space ima nultu toleranciju prema bilo kakvom sadržaju koji šteti, iskorištava ili ugrožava djecu. Odmah ćemo prijaviti policiji i organizacijama za zaštitu djece svakoga tko pokuša dijeliti takav materijal.

---

## 1. Zero Tolerance (Nulta tolerancija)

We strictly prohibit:
- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** Any visual representation of children in sexually explicit situations.
- **Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (CSAE):** Any content depicting or facilitating child grooming, abuse, or trafficking.

Strogo zabranjujemo:
- **Materijal koji prikazuje seksualno zlostavljanje djece (CSAM):** Bilo koji vizualni prikaz djece u seksualno eksplicitnim situacijama.
- **Seksualno iskorištavanje i zlostavljanje djece (CSAE):** Bilo koji sadržaj koji prikazuje ili olakšava vabljenje (grooming), zlostavljanje ili trgovinu djecom.

---

## 2. How We Keep Children Safe (Kako štitimo djecu)

### On-Device Filtering (Filtriranje na uređaju)
* **Technical:** We utilize TensorFlow Lite models locally on the device to perform real-time image scanning and classification, blocking sensitive or inappropriate content from being loaded or shared.
* **Plain:** We have a smart detector running right on your phone. It checks pictures and blocks bad ones before they can be saved or sent to anyone else.

### Decentralized Blacklisting (Decentralizirana crna lista)
* **Technical:** Reported content hashes are combined using cryptographic token sets. When the number of unique report tokens exceeds **5% of the total interactions** on the post (with a minimum floor of 10 reports), the app executes a Client-Side Consensus takedown and automatically purges the content from the device.
* **Plain:** If you report a post, it gets marked. If enough people report it based on the post's popularity, the post is automatically deleted from every phone in the network.

### Law Enforcement Reporting (Prijavljivanje nadležnim tijelima)
* **Technical:** Verified reports of CSAM or CSAE submitted to our Firebase database will be reported immediately by the developer to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant regional law enforcement.
* **Plain:** The person who runs this app checks reports. If we find anyone trying to share something that harms children, we will send all details to child safety groups and the police immediately.

## 3. How to Report (Kako prijaviti)

If you see any content in the app that violates child safety rules, please report it immediately:
1. Tap the **Report** button under the post.
2. Select **Child Safety / CSAM / CSAE** as the reason.
3. Tap **Submit Report**.

Ako u aplikaciji vidite sadržaj koji krši pravila o sigurnosti djece, odmah ga prijavite:
1. Dodirnite gumb **Prijavi** (Report) ispod objave.
2. Odaberite **Sigurnost djece / CSAM / CSAE** kao razlog.
3. Dodirnite **Pošalji prijavu** (Submit Report).
