# Privacy Policy for AetherWorks

**Plain language:** This app does **not** collect any personal data about you. All data you create stays on your device unless you explicitly share it.

- **No tracking:** The app never sends analytics, ads, or usage statistics to any server.
- **Fictional profiles:** Any profile you create is a fictional character. You are asked not to use your real name or personal information.
- **Data storage:** All content is stored locally in an encrypted database (SQLCipher). The encryption key is derived from your password plus a hardware‑backed key stored in the Android Keystore.
- **Sharing:** When you turn the **Sharing Toggle** on, the app only exchanges *public* content with nearby devices using peer‑to‑peer connections. No personal identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, GPS location, etc.) are ever transmitted.
- **Export/Import:** You can export all your data to an encrypted `.aethex` file. The file is protected with a password you choose. The developer cannot recover this password.
- **Risk acknowledgment:** By using the app you acknowledge that you are doing so **entirely at your own risk**. The developer is not responsible for any loss of data, privacy breaches, or any other consequences.

For any questions about how your data is handled, refer to the **Disclaimer** in `agents.md`.
