AetherWorks is a decentralized content-sharing platform and social network with zero servers. All data lives on-device. The user has absolute sovereignty over what is shared, what is kept private, and who they interact with. Privacy is not a feature — it is the foundation.

**Core Features:**
* **Zero Servers:** There are no servers. There is no cloud. There is no backend. Every device is sovereign.
* **Privacy by Default:** The safest choice is always the one that collects the least. The app never asks for personal data. Profiles are fictional personas.
* **Offline First:** AetherWorks allows you to create, read, and manage your content fully offline. When you're ready, toggle the network to share with nearby peers.
* **End-to-End Encrypted Data:** All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. 
* **Anonymous Ratings:** Track the reputation of content through community feedback without ever identifying who voted using cryptographic tokens.
* **P2P Discovery:** Find other AetherWorks instances on nearby devices using Wi-Fi Direct, Wi-Fi Local Network, and Bluetooth.
